This Privacy Policy describes the privacy practices of **Multi Layout Manager** (the “Extension”), a Chrome browser extension developed by **[Your Name or Company Name]** (“we,” “us,” or “our”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling any information associated with your use of the Extension responsibly and transparently. By installing or using the Extension, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

### 1. Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information or usage data from you.**

The Extension operates entirely locally on your device. The only information associated with the Extension consists of browser layout data (such as window positions, sizes, tab arrangements, URLs, titles, and related metadata for multi-screen workspaces) that you choose to save. This data is stored **exclusively** in your browser’s local storage using the Chrome `chrome.storage` API.

- **No server-side collection**: We do not transmit, receive, or store any data on our servers or any third-party servers.
- **No analytics, tracking, or logging**: The Extension does not use cookies, pixels, beacons, analytics tools, crash reporting, or any other mechanism that would collect or send information about your browsing activity, device, or usage patterns.
- **No personal data**: We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, location data, or any other personally identifiable information.

All saved layouts remain under your sole control on your local device and are not accessible to us.

### 2. How We Use Information

Any layout data you save is used **solely** to provide the core functionality of the Extension: allowing you to save and restore browser window and tab layouts across multiple monitors and screens. The Extension accesses this locally stored data only when you explicitly trigger a save or load action.

We do not use this data for any other purpose, including advertising, profiling, research, or product improvement involving external parties.

### 3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose any data associated with the Extension to any third parties. Because no data leaves your browser, there is no disclosure to external entities.

We may disclose information only if required to do so by law (for example, in response to a valid subpoena or court order) or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public, but given the purely local nature of the data, such disclosures are extremely unlikely.

### 4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally in your Chrome browser’s storage. It is not encrypted beyond the protections provided by your browser and operating system. 

We strongly recommend that you use Chrome’s built-in sync features or backup mechanisms only if you understand that doing so may replicate the locally stored layout data across your signed-in devices. We are not responsible for the security of your browser or device.

While we design the Extension with security in mind and follow industry-standard practices for client-side extensions, we cannot guarantee absolute security of data stored in any browser environment. You are responsible for securing your device and browser.

### 5. Your Rights and Choices

- **Access, correction, and deletion**: You can view, modify, or delete all saved layouts directly within the Extension’s user interface or by clearing the Extension’s data in Chrome settings (`chrome://settings/content/siteData` or by managing extensions).
- **Uninstall**: Uninstalling the Extension will remove all associated local storage data.
- **Do Not Track**: The Extension does not track you and honors browser-level privacy signals where applicable.
